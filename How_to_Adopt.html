How to Adopt a Bun Bun

Adopting a bunny is a big deal. Bunnies, just like any other pet, require a lot of love and attention. When thinking about adopting a bunny, it is important to consider how much of your time you are willing/able to spend with your pet. Do you have the money and the resources to support your pet rabbit for as many as 14 years? Will you have time to feed, clean, groom, and play with your rabbit everyday? Do you have other pets that may take attention away from your rabbit? These are important questions to consider before jumping into adoption. That being said, if you are ready and willing to make a commitment to your future pet rabbit, both you and your bunny will be happier in each other's company.

  • Steps to Adoption

  • Step 1: Do some research. Know what to expect when it comes to raising a pet rabbit.
  • Step 2: Browse through our site. What kind of bunny will best match your personality or your family?
  • Step 3: Contact our staff.
  • Step 4: Schedule a visit to our shelter.
  • Step 5: Fill out that good ol' paperwork.
  • Step 6: Bring your bunny home!
