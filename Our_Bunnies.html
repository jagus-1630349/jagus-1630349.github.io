Meet Sir Chubs-a-lot

Sir Chubs-a-lot is one regal rabbit with a huge appetite. He can be seen feasting on his favorite vegetables at all hours of the day. He loves wearing his favorite fur-trimmed cape and will not go anywhere without it. Although he has a difficult time getting close to others, once he has earned your trust, he is a friendly and loyal rabbit that will surely stick by your side.
